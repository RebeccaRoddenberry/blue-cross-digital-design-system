Delivering Relevant Messages to Members via Customer Service On-Hold Line

Through a collaboration between the Marketing and Brand Engagement team and Customer Service Operations, ongoing strategy and content updates are planned to deliver relevant messages to members placed on hold when calling into customer service. The first of these updates rolled out on 12/2 to include messaging around flu shots, not delaying care and Blue Connect in both English and Spanish as well as new music.

Spanish EOBs

Commercial EOBs are now available in Spanish! The Bilingual Strategy and Content Team has worked to provide a better customer experience as well as lighten the load on our Customer Service Professionals. Now, instead of having to create Spanish EOBs through a manual process, if a member requests their EOBs in Spanish, that information can be coded in FACETS and their EOBs will automatically go out in Spanish from then on. It’s a major improvement for our Spanish-speaking customers.

- Ashley Ennis

Large Group Members can now opt-in to try the new Blue Connect 2.0 experience and State Health Plan has their very own co-branded experience. We look forward to getting valuable user feedback as we move all Group members to the new member portal by the first quarter of 2021.

The Provider Portal, Blue e, just recieved it's first release of several rounds of updates improving the user experience. While starting with the main navigation, new features will address how providers access member eligibility, find claims and so much more.

- Rebecca Roddenberry
