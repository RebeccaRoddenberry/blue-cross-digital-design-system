Understanding Health Insurance

Confused about deductibles, copays and coinsurance? Many people are. In fact, 52% of US consumers have what's called "low health care literacy." And our consumers are looking for help from us. The DotCom team is taking a Content Strategy approach to delivering a user-focused microsite with simple language, clear definitions, illustrated explanations, helpful tips, and interactive tools. We’ve taken baseline visitor traffic data, Google search, and competitor analysis, and a backlog of future topics to create a whole new learning experience. It doesn’t end there. We’re striving to continually improve the quality and quantity of these resources with guidance from user testing, MAG (our Member Advisory Group), and visitor feedback.

- Brian Wagner

Mallory Short and David Fisher with Digital Experience collaborated with many groups, including IT and Sales to provide an updated interface and better experience.

- Rebecca Roddenberry



Content Style Guide

The content team recently started the task of redoing the outdated Content Style Guide. This guide hadn’t been updated since 2014 and had many outdated and incorrect grammar and writing rules. The team worked for around 7 months on this project, and not only improved the overall usability of the guide, but added in sections to make writing our pieces easier, more consistent and more cohesive. The guide now contains testing resources, the Spanish language style guide, as well as, individual guides on how to write for different audiences and topics.

- Brian Wagner



All New Medicare Shopping Experience Ready for the Annual Enrollment Period

Medicare dot com has gotten a complete overhaul this year, just in time for Annual Enrollment. The new experience features clear paths to enrollment, a robust education section, and a fresh new design.

As a Medicare shopper navigates through the new Dot Com experience, they will be directed to see all plans available to them by using the Plan Finder Tool. We've already launched an experience for members to get a plan recommendation based on their plan usage. Now, we are adding the ability for non-members to get a recommendation as well, replacing what was formally known as "Rate Quote" for Medicare.

- Stacy Peterson & Rebecca Roddenberry

Diversity, Equity and Inclusion

At Blue Cross and Blue Shield of North Carolina (Blue Cross NC) our mission is to improve the health and wellbeing of our customers and communities through an unwavering commitment to quality, affordability and exceptional experience. For more than a decade we’ve been part of the collective conversation on how diversity shapes culture and success. Over the years, we’ve made great progress. We take pride in the culture we’ve built and the actions, policies and programs we’ve created to support it. But there’s still more to do.

From Dr. Sotunde’s message of hope to the new Inclusion and Racial Equity Challenges from David Laboy and Jeff Spain, we’ve built a microsite that spreads the word of our Diversity, Equity & Inclusion Resolve, Commitment and Promise.

Be sure to check out the Fearless for Good video where hundreds of employees gave thousands of hours and resources to diverse community organizations across our state.


- Brian Wagner
