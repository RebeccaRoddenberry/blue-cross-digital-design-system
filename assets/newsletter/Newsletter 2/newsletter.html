Fearless for Good

Our mission is to improve the health and well-being of our customers and communities. Our employees help us realize that mission by giving fearlessly of their time and resources to community organizations across the state. In 2019, our employees volunteered over 12,000 hours building bikes and playgrounds, packing backpacks, bundling diapers, and more.

"Fearless For Good" showcases all the great things we accomplished this year and how we're working to make things better.

Mobile Tech Talk

The Blue Connect Mobile digital experience team were featured recently in an IT TECHTalk article discussing their process for mobile app development at Blue Cross NC. Moshe Moryosef, the Emerging Technology Services Manager, sought to highlight the ways in which design can comfortably live side-by-side with development in an Agile Scrum environment.

The team’s design methodology was mapped out by Willow and Alan on a whiteboard and then was turned into an infographic suitable for the TECHTalk portal. The resulting infographic has already been used as a training tool for new Blue Connect Mobile team members.

Headline News is Breaking Records

2019 was a banner year for the new and improved Headline News! Starting in January, the team adopted a bold, new strategy that took the show from airing 8-10 pieces of content a year to delivering 52 unique pieces in 2019! This new approach took the tried and true Headline Market News brand and expanded it into an overarching flagship brand with a number of new and exciting sub-channels including HN Shorts, The Scoop with Gerald Petkau, and a monthly recap show where John Roos and Laura Eberhard round up and review the month’s top stories.
