Governmental

Along with our new Medicare Plan Finder tool, the Governmental team has spent a lot of time and effort this year educating our members about our Medicare offerings. Medicare Guided Selling Tool

Medicare is confusing – especially when members are moving from their employer’s coverage or an individual health insurance plan for the first time. That’s why we are launching our first ever Medicare Plan Finder. With this tool, current members can simply log in just like they do with Blue Connect and get an instant recommended Medicare plan based on their doctors, their prescriptions and where they live. The tool also allows members to compare other plans for added assurance that our recommended plan best fits their needs.

Brand Creative Team Supports Another Successful SPIRIT WEEK

In October, the Brand Creative Team produced over a dozen deliverables in support of Blue Cross NC's 3rd annual SPIRIT WEEK. Two of the most well-received items were new Fearless t-shirts and banners for the athletic field. Incorporating the Triune sculpture from the Durham headquarters campus, the t-shirts reinforce our mission and vision by emphasizing we're fearless about the future. The field banners also emphasize our mission to improve the health and wellbeing of our customers.
