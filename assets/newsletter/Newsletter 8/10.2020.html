WE RESOLVE Brand and Reputation Campaign

In response to the challenges that COVID-19 has brought on and magnified in our communities, we've created the WE RESOLVE campaign. Over the past two months we've released a new television commercial, radio spots, a YouTube video series, and web banners and social posts emphasizing that in these uncertain times Blue Cross NC is here for North Carolinians. These assets, which focus on community impact, behavioral health, and health care transformation, have received over 69 million impressions and driven almost 300,000 visits to our website. Upcoming WE RESOLVE assets will focus on open enrollment, understanding health insurance, racial equity, community investments, and more.

- Michael Pittman




OEP/AEP Home Page Refresh

WE RESOLVE. To make health care simpler. The DotCom team launched a refreshed, simpler home page on Oct .1 to meet not just our shopping consumers, but members and anyone seeking a better understanding of health insurance and care. Applying results from previous and ongoing user testing (300+ testers), we reduced the mental clutter by moving detailed content and links to relevant pages (such as the Member page), creating more space and providing easier choices. More importantly, we designed new interactive cards for shoppers to reveal specific, relevant and the most important details and links without leaving the page. Additionally, through collaborative content strategy and user testing, we are focusing on plan benefits and proof points (vs Blue Cross NC benefits) and what users care about when finding a plan such as preventive care, ER visits, managing their total health, and that COVID-19 will have a direct impact on how they shop this year.

- Brian Wagner



Health and Wellness Card Initiative

Starting October 19, Blue Cross NC will issue $200 million in health and wellness retail cards to more than 600,000 eligible subscribers, or households, across the state. The cards will be pre-loaded with funds that will help our members pay for health, wellness, and household expenses, including food, during the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic.

Members who are enrolled in individual under 65 and fully insured employer plans, including vision and dental plans, will receive a card.

Medicare.BlueCrossNC.Com

Just after midnight on Oct. 1, we launched an entirely new Medicare website and improved guided selling purchasing experience. Medicare.BlueCrossNC.com is a new one-stop home to educate and guide customers through to a plan that is right for their needs. The new website, which launched in time Annual Election Period (AEP), provides a better customer experience and positions us as a trusted advisor. The website also combines with our award-winning Medicare Plan Finder, which launched in January and is enhanced with this launch along with an improved online purchasing process. This tool offers current members aging into Medicare a plan recommendation based on what we already know about them, where they live, their medications and their claims information. In 2021, we plan to also offer a new public version of the tool that will use a few questions to guide all customers to a recommended plan option.

- Stacy Peterson
