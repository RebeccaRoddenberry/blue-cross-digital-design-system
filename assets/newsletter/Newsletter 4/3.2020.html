Our Response to COVID-19

As everyone is aware, the COVID-19 pandemic has been a huge global challenge. Fortunately, Blue Cross NC has risen to the occasion supporting its employees, members, and providers through this crisis. The Creative Strategy and Digital Experience Team has been at the forefront of this battle, creating member emails, direct mail pieces, Blue Connect display tiles, and updated webpages and infographics. We've translated important COVID-19 articles into Spanish. And we've also continued to provide Headline News to our employees while adhering to social distancing by using Skype for interviews.




User Testing Training

Thanks to UserZoom, our user research platform, digital experience professionals can run more user studies and run them much faster than ever before. UserZoom finds the participants for our studies and incentivizes them, removing much of the work required for gathering user feedback. In addition, UserZoom makes it possible to gather qualitative and quantitative data on participants’ ability to use our tools and on their perceptions of the tools. With this platform we can test public websites and prototypes of web sites or mobile apps. This capability makes it much easier for digital experience to obsess over data.

In the past few weeks, Leslie Carter has led several trainings for our digital experience team on UserZoom. The topics covered in the training will help our team be the expert partner regarding exceptional user experience. The training covered how to test information architecture with card sorts and tree tests. Formative usability test, which allow us to fail fast as we iterate on design, were covered. Also covered were benchmark usability test employed to compare the quality of one user experience to another.

Network Optimization B2B Campaign

The Q1 2020 B2B campaign for Major Group launched in early March. It includes a robust eBook and interactive infographic to help large employers strengthen the foundation of their health plan by optimizing their network. Traffic will be driven through multiple channels — from emails and direct mail, to promoted LinkedIn posts and an Outlook email signature. We can now cross-reference Adobe and Salesforce campaign IDs, so leads from the microsite contact form can be traced back to the specific marketing piece that brought them there.

Multimedia and Events Teams Deliver a Successful 2020 MVP Conference

The end of February brought out the MVPs of Sales and Marketing. This year’s theme was familiar to most: Star Trek and the Multimedia Team showed up strong and created custom video and animation work for the overarching two-day event.
