Blue Cross NC Statement on Racism and Traumatic Events

On June 3 after nationwide protests against systemic racism and recent traumatic events, Blue Cross NC issued a public statement condemning racism. Initially this was shared by our Communications team via a press release and social posts. However, the public dot com team felt the statement was worthy of a homepage takeover as well. Within 36 hours the team took down our normal homepage graphic and member login box and replaced it with a simple but powerfully designed header proclaiming "We Stand Against Racism." Over the next week 46,000 unique visitors saw this important message and hundreds clicked through to read the entire statement.

- Brian Wagner & Michael Pittman



Headline News

Since we closed our offices in March, Headline News has shifted both its delivery schedule and content in order to better reach and serve a work-from-home employee population. Within a week of employees working from home, HN adapted. HN went from delivering one piece per week to two, and narrowed our focus to primarily work on COVID and WFH-related stories.
During this time, HN also premiered a new show - HN: Cribs. HN: Cribs quickly started racking up the numbers and the episode featuring Torrey Swain became the most-viewed Headline News episode of all time with more than 1700 views.

Overall, HN viewership has skyrocketed during the first half of 2020. Average episode views have grown from about 200 per episode in May 2019 to more than 1000 per episode in May 2020.

Head over to Headline News’s Bloom Channel to see what all the fuss is about!

- Chad Danford



New Behavioral Health Site Goes Live

Improving access to behavioral health care — and improving the quality of that care — is one of our company’s top priorities. Yet we did not have much public-facing information outlining our efforts… Until now. Team Commercial worked closely with the Healthcare division to create a new microsite covering Blue Cross NC’s behavioral health strategy, tools and programs for providers, and mental health and substance use resources for members. We’ll continue adding new material to this site over time, as it will be the go-to destination for behavioral health. Kudos to Betsy Rack, George Lamontagne, Crystal Rovenstine and Jodi Ray for a lot of hard work to bring this to life!

- Jonathan Chaney



Spanish Translations for our COVID-19 Campaigns

The Bilingual Strategy and Content team has been working hard to get all of the Covid-related pages on the website translated into Spanish. We are partnering once again with our vendor, Motionpoint, to ensure this information is available to our Spanish-speaking members. We have also been collaborating with the bilingual CSPs to close some gaps that they had identified- for example, members now have the ability to fill out the Certificate of Coverage request form in Spanish on the website.

- Ashley Ennis



Blue Connect Mobile: User Testing Informs our Backlog

Our team has learned so much this year about User Testing and User Research with many thanks to Leslie Carter for her guidance along the way. Recently, Alan Tarrant and Walter Yang have been pushing the boundaries of the types of tests we run and expiramenting with the ways we summarize what we learn. Building with these user insights is making us a better Digital Experience team and providing a richer user experience for our users.
