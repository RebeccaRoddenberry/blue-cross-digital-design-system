Flush Left

When to Use the Flush Left Logo:
The flush left logo is the preferred configuration. It works well in horizontal applications where it can be proportionately scaled to fit the desired area. Cyan and black is the preferred color option. The cyan and white version is a preferred option if it’s on a dark solid background. If limited to one color, use the all-cyan, all-black or all-white version, depending on which works best in the design.

Centered

When to Use the Centered Logo (Stacked):
The centered logo is an optional configuration that allows the logo to be proportionately scaled larger than the Flush Left logo within a vertical or narrow application. Cyan and black is the preferred color option. The cyan and white version is a preferred option if it’s on a dark solid background. If limited to one color, use the all-cyan, all-black or all-white version, depending on which works best in the design.

Abbreviated


When to Use the Abbreviated Logo:
1. When the logo presence needs to be as large as possible in a defined space. + Where it needs to be identifiable from a far distance.

+ In very small spaces (like mobile viewpoints) where the lettering of the corporate logo would be too small to be readable.

+ When combined with numerous other logos, such as co-branding for State Health Plan, so that it stands out more clearly.

2. When we want to appear more approachable and casual. However the Cross Shield NC logo should never be used in a situation directly involving sales or insurance. The horizontal configuration is preferred. The stacked configuration may also be used when necessary.

Cyan and black is the preferred color option. The cyan and white logo version is a preferred option if placing the logo on a dark solid background. If limited to one color, use the centered stacked version.

Products

Please note: this is a small sample, all product logos included in the download

There are a number of other logos, or what we like to call type treatments, for BCBSNC products and programs. NOTE: Existing product and program type treatment files already exist. Therefore please use these files instead of recreating them. If a new plan or program type treatment is needed, always keep the following principles in mind when developing new ones:

1. The only use (other than in the standard BCBSNC logo) of the word “Blue” should be in product (plan) type treatments. The only time the font Walbaum Bold should be used is in the treatment of the word “Blue” when it is in a type treatment. The word “Blue” should always appear first and be Process Cyan. The second word should be Univers Light and be black.

2. Any product or program name that does not have the world “Blue” in it should be in Univers Light.

3. In most cases, when all the words are Univers Light, the first word should be Process Cyan and the second word black. 4. There are no character spaces between the words in type treatments. NOTE: When products or programs are mentioned in text, there should always be a character space between words.

5. Kerning should be manually tightened after converting the font to outlines. Use the above examples to gauge spacing.

Co-Branding and Joint Communications

Co-Branding is ANY tie to another brand that is public facing. This alliance associates a single product or service with more than one brand name and/or with a person. There are two types:

Joint Communications:

Any communication in which the Blue Cross and/or Blue Shield Brands and a third party’s name or brand appears together. Use Joint Communications to:

  • Align with BCBS marketing and communication efforts (e.g., sponsorships, etc.)
  • Support a business need (e.g., member rewards)
Logo Requirements:
  • Must be clear Blue Cross NC and other entity are separate companies
  • For sponsorships, the logo can be anywhere on the piece and any size

Co-Branded Communications:

A type of Joint Communication when a third party’s name or brand appears with the BCBS Brands AND the other company is engaging in the marketing, sale or administration of insurance or related services.

  • Communication is related to the marketing, sale or administration of BCBS Branded products
  • The other company is acting as a Support Company or Account Vendor, if you are not sure whether your organization falls into one of these category, please email your Blue Cross NC contact.
Logo Requirements:
  • Must be clear Blue Cross NC and other entity are separate companies
  • The other entity’s logo cannot be larger than Blue Cross NC’s and must be to the right of the Blue Cross NC brands
  • Appropriate disclosures are required
